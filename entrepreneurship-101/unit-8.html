Unit 8: People Skills for Nerds

Key Takeaways

  • Understand techniques to build stronger network relationships
  • Understand how to identify bad-faith actors
  • Understand how to effectively deescalate confrontation
  • Understand how to effectively enter into a negotiation
  • Learn how to rapidly build trust with new network connections
  • Learn how to objectively assess relationships and network connections
