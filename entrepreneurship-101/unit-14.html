Unit 14: Financial Planning

Key Takeaways

  • Learn key financial analysis skills (contribution analysis, financial analysis, accounting)
  • Learn key financial concepts (liquidity, value, taxation, contracts and warranties)
  • Learn how to evaluate business risk
  • Learn how to value information
  • Understand the minimum viable business product

Deliverables

  • Set up templates with instructional notes for contribution analysis, financial analysis, and simple startup accounting for your startup
  • Set up a cheat sheet on contracts, warranties, business planning, and taxation
  • Build a simple risk model
  • Evaluate the value of individual and networked user data captured within your startup’s MVP
  • Define your startup’s Minimum Viable Business Product
